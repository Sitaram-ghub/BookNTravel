    Cancellation Policy

    Last updated: May 2026

    1. General Policy

    At BookNTravel, we understand that plans change. You can cancel your booking through your dashboard or by contacting our customer support.

    2. Flight Cancellations

    Flights cancelled within 24 hours of booking will receive a full refund. Cancellations made less than 48 hours before departure are strictly non-refundable.

    3. Hotel & Package Cancellations

    You may cancel hotel reservations and tour packages up to 7 days before the check-in/start date for a 90% refund. Cancellations within 7 days will incur a 50% penalty fee.

    4. Refund Processing

    Refunds are processed back to the original payment method within 5-7 business days depending on your bank.
